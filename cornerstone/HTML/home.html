Welcome to Cornerstone Assembly

Loving God | Reaching People | Building Relationships

" God accepts you as you are. Not when you become acceptable. "

- Pastor Chris

At Cornerstone Assembly, we don't believe that you need to do anything special to be able to have a relationship with God. We are all growing and learning what it means to be like Jesus, and we would love for you to come and hang out with us. We meet at 4055 N 2nd St in Rogers on Sundays @ 10:00am and on Wednesdays @ 6:30pm.

Our team at Cornerstone Assembly

Chris & CaSandra Brewer

Chris Brewer is our lead pastor at Cornerstone Assembly, and CaSandra Brewer is our children's pastor. Both of them work very hard for our church and minister to the congregation with love and compassion.

Nathaniel Leyva

Nathaniel is our worship leader. He is a music major at the University of Arkansas and brings his talent and skill to our worship at Cornerstone Assembly. He works hard to bring great sounding, spirit-led worship to our church.

Javan Cleghorn

Javan is our youth pastor and the leader of the media team at Cornerstone. He takes care of everything from the sound during service to planning awesome retreats and events for our youth group.

Angela Cleghorn

Angela is also a leader of our youth group, and she is a huge part of our children's ministry on Sunday mornings. You will catch Angela serving anywhere and everywhere, including on the worship team.
