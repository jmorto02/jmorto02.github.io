About Us

an Assemblies of God church in Rogers, AR.


We are Cornerstone Assembly. Cornerstone isn't a building, and it isn't a location. Our church is a group of people, a family that meets solely to grow in our Christian faith, to worship Jesus, and to encourage each other.

We meet at 4055 N 2nd St in Rogers on Sundays @ 10am and on Wednesdays @ 6:30pm. We don't expect anyone to be perfect in order to come worship with us, and we don't have any sort of dress code. We just hope that you come with an open heart and expecting to have a good time!
